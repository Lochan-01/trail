About VIDYUTH

VIDYUTH is an annual cultural and sports festival held at our university, bringing together students from all over the country to participate in a wide range of activities. The festival aims to promote creativity, collaboration, and sportsmanship through various events and competitions. It is a platform where students showcase their talents and passions.

Events

VIDYUTH features a diverse range of events, including cultural performances, music and dance competitions, drama, debates, and more. Alongside cultural activities, the sports segment brings in various sporting events, from athletics to team sports. The events are designed to encourage participation, creativity, and healthy competition among students.

Event Schedule

The festival runs over three days, with different events taking place throughout the day. The schedule includes performances, competitions, workshops, and special sessions with guest speakers. The full schedule will be available on the official festival website as the event date approaches, so participants can plan accordingly.
